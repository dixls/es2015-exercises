

    What is the difference between var and let?
        var is scoped to a function, let is scoped to a code block, also let cannot be redeclared
    What is the difference between var and const?
        similar to let, but additionally const cannot be reassigned
    What is the difference between let and const?
        let can be reassigned, const cannot
    What is hoisting?
        variable defined with var are initialized at the beginning of the code, even if they are not defined, meaning they can be accessed without having a definition before they are actually declared
